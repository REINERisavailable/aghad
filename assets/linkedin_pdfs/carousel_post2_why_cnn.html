Step 1: The Sliding Window

Imagine reading a book with a magnifying glass.

The AI does exactly this. It slides a "window" across an image to look for basic patterns, like simple lines and curves.

Swipe 👉

Step 2: Building the Big Picture

Layer 1 finds lines.

Layer 2 combines lines into shapes.

Layer 3 combines shapes into faces, cars, or trees!

It's like building with LEGO blocks.

Swipe 👉

The Secret Weapon: Weight Sharing

If the AI learns what a dog looks like in the top corner of a photo, it instantly knows how to find a dog in the bottom corner too.

It doesn't have to re-learn it!
