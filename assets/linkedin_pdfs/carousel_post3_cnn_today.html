The Big Problem

Training an AI from scratch to understand the world takes weeks, millions of images, and supercomputers.

Most of us don't have that kind of time or money.

Swipe 👉

The Solution: Transfer Learning

Instead of starting from zero, we download an AI that Google or Meta already spent weeks training.

It already knows how to see edges, curves, and shapes!

Swipe 👉

Don't Reinvent the Wheel

We take their super-smart AI, freeze its "eyes", and just train it for a few minutes on our specific task.

(Like teaching a really smart adult a new language, instead of teaching a baby).
