The Dark Ages (Before CNNs)

Imagine trying to teach a computer what a "cat" is by manually typing out the math for pointy ears, whiskers, and fur.

It was exhausting, inaccurate, and didn't work if the cat was upside down!

Swipe 👉

Enter the CNN

CNN stands for "Convolutional Neural Network."

Don't let the scary name fool you. It just means the computer learned to scan images like a human eye does.

Swipe 👉

How is it different?

Instead of humans doing the hard work, we just show the CNN thousands of pictures of cats.

It automatically figures out the rules. It teaches itself what "fur" and "whiskers" look like!
