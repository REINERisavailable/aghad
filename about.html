Why I Help Students & Career Changers

Simple: because there's no clear playbook out there.

My journey started in my first year of high school. I stumbled upon Andrew Ng's famous Machine Learning course and started learning Python. Back then, everything felt overwhelmingly theoretical and complex.

As I grew and eventually entered my Master's program, I realized a hard truth:

  • You can read all the complex math papers in the world, but if you don't know how to deploy a simple model or write clean code, your project will fail.
  • You might understand how a Convolutional Neural Network works under the hood, but without proper communication skills, business stakeholders won't care.
  • The AI landscape moves too fast. Tools drop every single day. People are overwhelmed and paralyzed by choices.

I wanted to fix that. I wanted to build the bridge between "complex academic AI" and "practical, real-world skills" for normal people trying to enter this amazing field. 😁
